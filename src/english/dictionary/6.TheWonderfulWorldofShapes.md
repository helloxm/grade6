## 课文

Our world is full of different **shapes**. We can see them everywhere.

The sun looks like a **circle**. Our books are often **rectangle**. A piece of bread might be a **square**. When I draw a house, I use a **triangle** for the roof. An egg has an **oval** shape.

I love to see a **star** in the sky at night. On Valentine's Day, people send cards with a **heart** shape. In some games, there is a **diamond** shape. A stop sign is a **polygon** with eight sides, which is called an octagon.

Some shapes are not flat. A soda can is like a **cylinder**. Our planet Earth is a **sphere**. We play with **cube** blocks. An ice cream cone is a **cone**.

A spring looks like a **spiral**. The moon sometimes appears in a **crescent** shape. A beehive has many **hexagon** cells.

Shapes make our world beautiful and interesting. Learning about shapes is fun!

**奇妙的形状世界**

我们的世界充满了不同的**形状**。我们到处都能看到它们。

太阳看起来像一个**圆形**。我们的书通常是**长方形**的。一片面包可能是**正方形**的。当我画房子时，我用一个**三角形**做屋顶。鸡蛋有一个**椭圆形**。

我喜欢在夜晚看天上的**星星**。在情人节，人们会送**心形**的卡片。在一些游戏中，有**菱形**。停车标志是一个有八条边的**多边形**，叫做八边形。

有些形状不是平面的。苏打罐像一个**圆柱体**。我们的星球地球是一个**球体**。我们玩**立方体**积木。冰淇淋蛋筒是一个**圆锥体**。

弹簧看起来像**螺旋形**。月亮有时会呈现**新月形**。蜂巢有许多**六边形**的巢室。

形状使我们的世界美丽而有趣。学习形状很有趣！
