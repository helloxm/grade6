## 课文

Last summer, my family took a trip to explore the beauty of nature. We first climbed a tall **mountain**. At the top, we could see a wide **river** flowing into the blue **ocean** far away. Below us was a vast **forest** with many tall **tree**s.

In the **forest**, we saw all kinds of **flower**s, including a beautiful red **rose**. Green **grass** covered the ground. I picked up a **seed** from a plant to plant at home. Looking up at the **sky**, we saw white **cloud**s. The **sun** was shining brightly. We stayed until evening to see the **moon** and the **star**s appear. After a brief **rain** shower, a colorful **rainbow** appeared in the **sky**!

We took a deep breath of the fresh **air**. The **wind** was blowing gently. We drank some clean **water** from a **stream** and later made a **fire** to keep warm.

The next day, we visited a **desert** area and then took a boat to a small **island**. We swam in a clear **lake** and saw a magnificent **waterfall**. We also walked along a **beach** and collected pretty **stone**s. It was a wonderful journey that helped us appreciate our amazing natural world.

## 穿越自然之旅

去年夏天，我们全家进行了一次旅行，去探索大自然的美丽。我们首先爬上了一座高大的**大山**。在山顶，我们可以看到一条宽阔的**河流**流入远方的蓝色**海洋**。在我们脚下是一片广阔的**森林**，里面有许多高大的**树**。

在**森林**里，我们看到了各种各样的**花**，包括一朵美丽的红色**玫瑰**。绿色的**草**覆盖着地面。我从一株植物上捡起一颗**种子**，准备带回家种植。抬头望向**天空**，我们看到了白色的**云**。**太阳**明亮地照耀着。我们一直待到晚上，看**月亮**和**星星**出现。一场短暂的**雨**过后，一道五彩缤纷的**彩虹**出现在**天空**中！

我们深深呼吸着新鲜的**空气**。**风**轻轻地吹着。我们从小**溪**里喝了一些干净的**水**，后来还生了一堆**火**来取暖。

第二天，我们参观了一个**沙漠**地区，然后乘船去了一个**小岛**。我们在清澈的**湖泊**里游泳，看到了壮观的**瀑布**。我们还沿着**海滩**散步，收集漂亮的**石头**。这是一次美妙的旅程，让我们更加欣赏我们神奇的自然世界。
