## 课文

In our town, everyone has an important **occupation**. My mother is a **teacher** and my father is an **engineer**. They love their jobs. When I was sick yesterday, a friendly **doctor** and a kind **nurse** took care of me at the clinic. Next door, our neighbor, a **dentist**, reminded me to brush my teeth.

On my way home, I saw a **policeman** directing traffic and a **firefighter** washing the fire truck. A **postman** was delivering letters, and a **cleaner** was keeping our streets spotless. I stopped at a new building site where an **architect** was looking at plans, and a **mechanic** was fixing a big machine.

I then went to the bank with my uncle, who is a **banker**. The **accountant** there was very busy. For lunch, we went to a restaurant. The **chef** cooked delicious food, and the **waiter** served us with a smile. My aunt, a **lawyer**, joined us and talked about her new case.

My cousin dreams of being a **pilot** or a **scientist**. I have other friends too: one is a **singer**, another is an **artist** who paints beautiful pictures, and my brother is an **actor** in the school play, while his friend is a talented **actress**. My sister wants to be a **writer**, and my grandfather, a **farmer**, sends us fresh vegetables. We also met a **salesperson** showing new products and a **driver** waiting for his next trip. Everyone, from the **assistant** in the shop to all the others, makes our town a wonderful place to live. Every job is valuable.
I love all these festivals because they bring families and friends together.

## 我们小镇的一天

在我们的小镇上，每个人都有一份重要的**职业**。我的妈妈是一名**教师**，爸爸是一名**工程师**。他们都很热爱自己的工作。昨天我生病时，一位友好的**医生**和一位善良的**护士**在诊所照顾我。隔壁的邻居是一位**牙医**，他提醒我要刷牙。

在回家的路上，我看到一名**警察**在指挥交通，一名**消防员**在清洗消防车。一位**邮递员**正在投递信件，一位**清洁工**正在保持我们的街道一尘不染。我在一个新建筑工地停下，那里有一位**建筑师**在看图纸，一位**机械师**在修理一台大机器。

然后我和我的**银行家**叔叔去了银行。那里的**会计**非常忙碌。午餐时间，我们去了一家餐厅。**厨师**烹饪了美味的食物，**服务生**微笑着为我们服务。我的**律师**阿姨也来了，并谈论她的新案子。

我的表哥梦想成为一名**飞行员**或**科学家**。我还有其他朋友：一个是**歌唱家**，另一个是画美丽图画的**画家**，我的哥哥是学校话剧中的一名**男演员**，而他的朋友是一位有才华的**女演员**。我的姐姐想成为一名**作家**，我的**农民**祖父给我们送来新鲜蔬菜。我们还遇到一位展示新产品的**销售员**和一位等待下一次出车的**司机**。每个人，从店里的**助理**到所有其他人，都让我们的小镇成为一个美好的家园。每一份工作都是有价值的。
