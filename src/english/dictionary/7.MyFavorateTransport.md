## 课文

There are many kinds of transportation in the world. They help us go from one place to another.

I usually go to school by **bus**. Sometimes, my father drives me in his **car**. On weekends, I ride my **bicycle** to the park. Last summer, my family traveled to another city by **train**. It was comfortable and fast.

My uncle has a **motorcycle**. It looks very cool. I have never ridden in a **helicopter** or an **airplane**, but I hope to fly one day. My grandfather took a **ship** when he was young. He said it was a long trip on the sea.

In big cities, many people take the **subway** because it is not crowded. A **taxi** is easy to take, but it costs more. A **truck** is big and strong. It can carry heavy things. A **van** can carry many people, just like a small **bus**.

I saw a **ferry** on TV. It takes people across the river. Rich people may have a **yacht** to sail on the sea. A **jeep** is good for driving on mountains. The most amazing transportation is the **spaceship**. It can fly to space!

I like all these transports. They make our life convenient and interesting.

**中文翻译参考：**

**我最喜欢的交通工具**

世界上有很多种交通工具。它们帮助我们从一个地方去往另一个地方。

我通常乘**公共汽车**去上学。有时候，爸爸会用他的**汽车**送我。周末我会骑**自行车**去公园。去年夏天，我们全家坐**火车**去另一个城市旅行。火车既舒适又快速。

我叔叔有一辆**摩托车**，它看起来非常酷。我从未坐过**直升机**或**飞机**，但我希望有一天能乘坐。我的祖父年轻时坐过**轮船**，他说那是一次漫长的海上旅行。

在大城市里，很多人乘坐**地铁**，因为地铁不拥挤。**出租车**很容易搭乘，但价格更贵。**卡车**又大又结实，能运送很重的东西。**面包车**可以载很多人，就像一辆小型的**公共汽车**。

我在电视上看过**渡轮**，它载着人们过河。富人可能拥有**游艇**在海上航行。**吉普车**很适合在山地上行驶。最令人惊叹的交通工具是**宇宙飞船**，它能飞向太空！

我喜欢所有这些交通工具。它们让我们的生活变得方便而有趣。
