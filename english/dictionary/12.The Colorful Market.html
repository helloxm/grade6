12.The Colorful Market

课文

My grandmother and I went to the market last weekend. It was full of colorful fruits and vegetables. First, we bought some fruits: a red apple, a yellow banana, a green pear, and a juicy orange. For a special treat, we got a big watermelon, a bunch of purple grapes, and some red cherries. We also picked up a sweet mango, a box of fresh strawberries, and a soft peach. Grandmother chose a lemon for her tea, a pineapple for dessert, and a kiwifruit for our breakfast. She even bought a coconut and a durian to try something new. Finally, she picked up a large pomelo.

Next, we moved to the vegetable section. We bought some long green beans and round peas. We needed a pumpkin for soup, an onion for cooking, and a fresh cucumber for salad. We also picked up a purple eggplant, several potatoes, and a green cabbage. For making sauce, we chose ripe tomatoes. We didn't forget the orange carrots and the sweet potato for a healthy snack.

Our bags were heavy, but we were happy. We love eating all kinds of fruits and vegetables because they are healthy and delicious.

五彩缤纷的市场

上周末，我和奶奶去了市场。那里到处都是五彩缤纷的水果和蔬菜。首先，我们买了一些水果：一个红苹果，一个黄香蕉，一个绿梨和一个多汁的橙子。为了犒劳自己，我们买了一个大西瓜，一串紫葡萄和一些红樱桃。我们还挑了一个香甜的芒果，一盒新鲜的草莓和一个软软的桃。奶奶为她的茶选了一个柠檬，为甜点选了一个菠萝，为我们的早餐选了一个猕猴桃。她甚至还买了一个椰子和一个榴莲来尝试新东西。最后，她挑了一个大柚子。

接着，我们来到了蔬菜区。我们买了一些长长的青豆和圆圆的豌豆。我们需要一个做汤的南瓜，一个做菜的洋葱和一根做沙拉的新鲜黄瓜。我们还拿了一个紫茄子，几个土豆和一个绿卷心菜。为了做酱汁，我们选了成熟的西红柿。我们也没有忘记橙色的胡萝卜和健康的零食红薯。

我们的袋子很重，但我们很开心。我们喜欢吃各种水果和蔬菜，因为它们既健康又美味。
