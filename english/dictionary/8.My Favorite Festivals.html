8.My Favorite Festivals

课文

There are many festivals around the world. They bring us joy and happiness.

My favorite festival is the Spring Festival. It is the most important festival in China. We eat dumplings and get red envelopes. The Lantern Festival comes after it. We eat sweet rice balls and watch beautiful lanterns.

The Dragon Boat Festival is fun. We eat zongzi and watch dragon boat races. The Mid-Autumn Festival is in autumn. We eat mooncakes and admire the full moon.

I also like some Western festivals. Christmas is in December. People decorate Christmas trees and give gifts. Thanksgiving is a time to be thankful. Families eat turkey together. Easter is in spring. Children look for Easter eggs.

There are also special days for people. Children's Day is for kids. Teachers' Day honors our teachers. Women's Day, Mother's Day, and Father's Day thank important people in our lives. Labor Day thanks workers. National Day celebrates our country.

New Year starts on January 1st. Valentine's Day is for love.

I love all these festivals because they bring families and friends together.

中文翻译参考：

我最喜欢的节日

世界上有很多节日。它们带给我们快乐和幸福。

我最喜欢的节日是春节。它是中国最重要的节日。我们吃饺子并收红包。元宵节在春节之后到来。我们吃汤圆，看美丽的花灯。

端午节很有趣。我们吃粽子，看龙舟比赛。中秋节在秋天。我们吃月饼，赏满月。

我也喜欢一些西方节日。圣诞节在十二月。人们装饰圣诞树并互赠礼物。感恩节是表达感谢的时刻。家人们一起吃火鸡。复活节在春天。孩子们寻找复活节彩蛋。

还有一些为特定人群设立的特别日子。儿童节是给孩子们的。教师节向我们的老师致敬。妇女节、母亲节和父亲节感谢我们生命中重要的人。劳动节感谢劳动者。国庆节庆祝我们的国家。

新年从 1 月 1 日开始。情人节是关于爱的节日。

我喜欢所有这些节日，因为它们让家人和朋友团聚在一起。
