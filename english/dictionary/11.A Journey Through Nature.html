11.A Journey Through Nature

课文

Last summer, my family took a trip to explore the beauty of nature. We first climbed a tall mountain. At the top, we could see a wide river flowing into the blue ocean far away. Below us was a vast forest with many tall trees.

In the forest, we saw all kinds of flowers, including a beautiful red rose. Green grass covered the ground. I picked up a seed from a plant to plant at home. Looking up at the sky, we saw white clouds. The sun was shining brightly. We stayed until evening to see the moon and the stars appear. After a brief rain shower, a colorful rainbow appeared in the sky!

We took a deep breath of the fresh air. The wind was blowing gently. We drank some clean water from a stream and later made a fire to keep warm.

The next day, we visited a desert area and then took a boat to a small island. We swam in a clear lake and saw a magnificent waterfall. We also walked along a beach and collected pretty stones. It was a wonderful journey that helped us appreciate our amazing natural world.

穿越自然之旅

去年夏天，我们全家进行了一次旅行，去探索大自然的美丽。我们首先爬上了一座高大的大山。在山顶，我们可以看到一条宽阔的河流流入远方的蓝色海洋。在我们脚下是一片广阔的森林，里面有许多高大的树。

在森林里，我们看到了各种各样的花，包括一朵美丽的红色玫瑰。绿色的草覆盖着地面。我从一株植物上捡起一颗种子，准备带回家种植。抬头望向天空，我们看到了白色的云。太阳明亮地照耀着。我们一直待到晚上，看月亮和星星出现。一场短暂的雨过后，一道五彩缤纷的彩虹出现在天空中！

我们深深呼吸着新鲜的空气。风轻轻地吹着。我们从小溪里喝了一些干净的水，后来还生了一堆火来取暖。

第二天，我们参观了一个沙漠地区，然后乘船去了一个小岛。我们在清澈的湖泊里游泳，看到了壮观的瀑布。我们还沿着海滩散步，收集漂亮的石头。这是一次美妙的旅程，让我们更加欣赏我们神奇的自然世界。
